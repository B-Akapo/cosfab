Who We Are

Welcome to Cosfab, an interior design firm based in Lagos, Nigeria, but providing services across the continent. Our story begins with a passion for creating beauty that brings peace of mind. Our founder, Cosmas Maduka, established cosfab to provide homeowners with the luxury they crave and deserve.

We understand the importance of creating a serene and beautiful home and we specialize in transforming your living space into a safe haven where you can rest and truly call it home. We are dedicated to providing you with the satisfaction that makes you say "ahhhh."

Our team of talented designers will work closely with you to understand your vision and bring it to life. We specialize in creating custom designs that are tailored to your unique style and preferences. Whether you're looking to renovate a single room or an entire home, we have the skills and expertise to deliver a stunning result.

We are proud to have helped many homeowners across the continent create their dream homes, and we would be honored to do the same for you. Contact us today to schedule a consultation, and let's turn your vision into reality.
